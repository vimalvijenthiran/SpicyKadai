ABOUT US

It all started back in 2018 After our graduation.We decided to make life easier by changing the way Srilanka eats- all with just a tap! With our idea of 'Spicy foods delivery',all we needed was the tech to power it and were introducted,who brought this vision to life with the first website. And with this,spicy Kadai was launched as a food ordering & delivery platform.

Today spicy kadai is a leading food ordering and delivery startup in Srilanka.The innovative technology, large and nimble delivery service, and exceptional consumer focus at spicy food enabled a host of benefits that includes lightning fast deliveries, live order tracking and no restrictions in the quantity of orders you place, all while having the pleasure of enjoying your favourite meal wherever you'd like it. Our future depends in your hands ....Keep supporting us and enjoy your life with the more spice of our recipes!
